## מטלת הגשה

**שני תוצרים. מה שנאמר - אין קיצורים.**

**תוצר 1 - Transformation Plan (3-5 עמודים):**
- Executive Summary
- Where We Are (current state)
- Where We're Going (vision + goals)
- How We Get There (3 Use Cases + Roadmap)
- Governance Framework
- Change Management Plan

**תוצר 2 - Executive Deck (10 שקפים):**
שקפים: (1) Why Now (2) Opportunity (3) What We've Done (4) Proposed Use Cases (5) Business Case (6) Risks and Mitigations (7) Roadmap (8) Resources Required (9) Change Management (10) Call to Action
